Welcome to my page

The Synthwave edition!

I started at Sofology in 2018.

I began as a webchat agent dealing with various different queries in regards to sofa sizes, styles, colours and so on. I really enjoyed my job, however it soon became more Sales based. (Which I am terrible at!) This prompted me to broaden my horizons and really think about the career path I wanted to go down. I've been in Customer Services all my life so a change was definitely needed!

Sofology began to offer Junior Developer Apprenticeships, with no experience and a pocket full of hope, I decided to apply. 2 Group exercises later and a nerve-wracking week of waiting, I found out that I had the role!

Now, here I am, currently on an intense Bootcamp with the help of CodeNation! Living my best life! I can't wait to hone and develop my skills further with other developers out there.
